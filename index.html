Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some common things clients ask us.

What is your process for starting a new project?

Our process begins with a discovery call to understand your vision, goals, and requirements. We then move to requirement analysis, design, development, testing, and finally, deployment. We believe in transparent communication and keep you updated at every stage.

How long does a typical website project take?

The timeline for a website project varies depending on its complexity, features, and the extent of custom work required. A typical business website can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks from start to finish. We'll provide a detailed project timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide support after the project is launched?

Absolutely! We offer various post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure your website or application remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. We're here to be your long-term technology partner.

How do you handle project communication and updates?

We assign a dedicated project manager to each project who serves as your primary point of contact. We use tools like Slack, Jira, and regular email updates to ensure you are always in the loop. We also schedule weekly or bi-weekly check-in calls to discuss progress and gather feedback.
